## 💡 Key Insights

### Git is Simple
At its core, Git is just 3 steps:
1. Edit files
2. Stage changes (git add)
3. Create snapshot (git commit)

Everything else builds on this!

### Learning Takes Practice
- Reading is 10% of learning
- Doing is 90% of learning
- That's why exercises are crucial!

### Mistakes Are Good
- Mistakes teach you more than success
- That's why Exercise 4 teaches recovery
- You can't break anything permanently

### Your Roadmap is Flexible
- You can go faster or slower
- Skip advanced topics if you want
- Come back to anything anytime

### AIML Makes You Special

- Your guides have both!
